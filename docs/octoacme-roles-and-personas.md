# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Sponsor / Executive Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Sponsors are executive-level stakeholders who provide business context, prioritization authority, and escalation oversight for major initiatives. They ensure projects align with strategic objectives and remove organizational barriers.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success criteria at the strategic level
- Approve project initiation, scope changes, and major resource decisions
- Provide escalation path for Level 3 blockers and business-impacting issues
- Champion the project internally and secure necessary organizational support
- Review milestone progress and validate ROI against initial objectives

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Minimize organizational friction and resource conflicts
- Maintain strategic alignment across initiatives

### Typical Communication
- Monthly or milestone-based executive reviews
- Escalation path for critical decisions
- Approval gates at initiation and major milestones

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Works with PM** on project charter approval, milestone reviews, and escalations
- **Receives updates from Product Manager** on success metrics and strategic alignment
- **Final decision authority** for scope trade-offs and priority conflicts

---

## QA / Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA and Testing Leads design and execute comprehensive testing strategies to ensure quality and acceptance. They collaborate with developers and product teams to validate that features meet requirements and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Create test plans aligned with acceptance criteria
- Design and execute manual and automated test cases
- Participate in acceptance criteria refinement during planning
- Lead quality gate reviews before release
- Identify and document defects with clear reproduction steps
- Contribute to Definition of Done criteria

### Goals
- Ensure all deliverables meet quality standards before release
- Reduce production defects and customer-impacting bugs
- Validate that acceptance criteria are objectively met

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and acceptance criteria alignment meetings
- QA status in daily standups and weekly syncs
- Test reports and quality metrics reviews
- Pre-release quality gates

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Collaborates with Developers** on test automation, edge cases, and defect resolution
- **Works with Product Manager** on acceptance criteria clarity and priority of test scenarios
- **Supports Project Manager** in defining quality gates and release readiness

---

## Technical Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide architectural guidance, design reviews, and technical risk management. They ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with technical standards.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical approach and architecture for major features
- Conduct design reviews and approve technical decisions
- Identify technical risks and propose mitigation strategies
- Provide guidance on technology choices and trade-offs
- Mentor developers and ensure code quality standards
- Participate in estimation to surface technical complexity

### Goals
- Deliver technically sound, maintainable solutions
- Reduce technical debt and architectural risks
- Enable team scalability and knowledge sharing

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and technical sync meetings
- Code review participation and feedback
- Risk register and technical dependency tracking
- Architecture documentation and decision logs

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Guides Developers** through design decisions and technical standards
- **Advises Product Manager** on technical feasibility and timeline impact
- **Partners with Project Manager** on technical risk tracking and dependency management

---

## Scrum Master / Process Facilitator

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters (in Agile environments) or Process Facilitators facilitate team ceremonies, remove process blockers, and ensure consistent application of project management practices. They coach the team on process adherence and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, planning, and retrospective meetings
- Enforce process consistency and ceremony participation
- Remove process-related blockers and impediments
- Coach team members on Agile/project management best practices
- Track metrics (velocity, burndown) and highlight trends
- Ensure Definition of Done is understood and applied

### Goals
- Maintain process discipline and predictability
- Maximize team efficiency and reduce meeting overhead
- Foster continuous improvement culture

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standups
- Weekly retrospective facilitation
- Process improvement recommendations
- Metrics and trend reporting

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Coaches all team members** on process adherence and best practices
- **Supports Project Manager** in executing ceremonies and tracking progress
- **Escalates process blockers** to Project Manager for resolution

---

## Stakeholder / Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Business Analysts act as the voice of the customer and business. They translate business requirements into actionable insights, gather feedback, and ensure solutions address real user needs and business problems.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements and user needs
- Validate that solutions align with business and user expectations
- Provide user feedback and usability insights
- Clarify acceptance criteria and success metrics with customers/users
- Escalate requirement changes and scope issues
- Participate in acceptance testing and user validation

### Goals
- Ensure solutions solve real business and user problems
- Reduce rework due to misaligned requirements
- Maximize customer satisfaction and adoption

### Typical Communication
- Requirements gathering sessions and user interviews
- Acceptance criteria review and clarification
- User feedback collection and synthesis
- Stakeholder update meetings

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Partners with Product Manager** on requirements validation and user feedback synthesis
- **Collaborates with Project Manager** on scope management and requirement changes
- **Provides QA/Testing team** with user scenarios and acceptance criteria clarity
- **Updates Sponsor** on stakeholder concerns and business alignment
